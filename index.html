I was born in the US, but I moved around a lot as a child. Before college I lived in Russia the longest, 10 years.

I moved back to the US to attend Taylor University where I got my B.A. in Creative Writing. After college I had several jobs (including floor installer) and eventually found myself in Tennessee where I attended Lee University and got an MAT for English 6-12.

I taught middle school English at Dalewood Middle School in Chattanooga for a year before moving back to Indiana. After COVID hit, I decided to broaden my skill set. Eventually, I began studying Web Development at Eleven Fifty Academy. While there I discovered my passion for coding and logical problem solving.
